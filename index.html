About Origami

Origami is the traditional Japanese art of paper folding, which transforms a flat sheet of paper into a finished sculpture through folding and sculpting techniques. This art form is known for its beautiful and intricate designs, ranging from simple shapes to complex structures.

How to Make an Origami Crane

Step 1: Start with a Square Piece of Paper

Begin with a square piece of origami paper, colored side up.

Step 2: Fold Diagonally

Fold the paper diagonally in half to form a triangle, then unfold.

Step 3: Fold the Other Diagonal

Fold the paper diagonally in the other direction, then unfold again.

Step 4: Fold the Corners

Fold the top corners down to the center crease line.

Step 5: Create the Kite Base

Fold the top of the triangle down to the bottom point.

Step 6: Fold the Bottom Up

Fold the bottom corner of the kite shape up to the top point.

Step 7: Create the Wings

Fold the sides down to form the wings of the crane.

Step 8: Shape the Head and Tail

Fold one tip down to form the head and the other tip upwards for the tail.

Step 9: Finished Origami Crane

Your origami crane is complete! You can adjust the wings and tail for a more realistic look.
